Welcome to Iqbal Turmeric Traders in Changa Manga, where we have been proudly serving our customers for over 10 years. We specialize in providing high-quality, pure turmeric that is grown and processed with care. Our goal is to deliver the best natural product that adds color, flavor, and health benefits to your life.


We mainly deal through cash and bank transfers, making it easy and simple for our local customers to do business with us. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with our buyers based on trust, quality, and honesty.


Our turmeric is known for its rich color, natural aroma, and purity. We believe in keeping things simple — no middlemen, no compromise, just genuine turmeric straight from our farms to you.


We thank our loyal customers who have supported us throughout this journey, and we look forward to welcoming new ones. Whether you are a local shop, a wholesaler, or an individual buyer, we are here to serve you with the best turmeric from Changa Manga.
